Easy multi-channel payment integrations for your Woocommerce store.


== Description ==

Integrating Watupay into your Woocommerce store means you can accept payment via multiple channels (card, ussd, bank transfer and QR code) from multiple countries.

Your customers will find it easier to pay you. You'll boost sales and be able to access your funds at anytime.

Features:

* Credit Card Payment
* Watu Platform Payment
* Bank USSD Payment
* Bank Transfer Payment
* Mobile Money Payment


== Installation ==

This plugin requires WooCommerce.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Go to Woocommerce settings -> Payments.
4. Setup Watupay by inputting your merchant parameters. Enable Watupay.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is WatuPay? =

Watupay is the most convenient way to accept payments through multiple platforms. Experience Realtime Payment Integrations on Web and Mobile and boost your business sales with our certified and trusted payment integrations available for small to complex business projects.

= How can I start using Watupay? =

Create a personal or business account at https://dashboard.watupay.com/register , verify your email address and you are ready to go in a matter of minutes.

= Do I pay for using WatuPay on my site? =

No, Watupay is free. You only need to sign up and verify your account with the required documents.

= What are the modes of Payment available on WatuPay? =

Available modes of payment are dependent on the country your user is paying from. They could include Debit/Credit cards, USSD, Bank Transfer, Bank OTP, Mobile Money, WatuWallet, etc.
